Why Tudinous Kryos

Economic, reliable & fit to purpose

Cryogenic work is full of uncertainty: component failure, cold losses, repeated experimentation. That makes budget matter: budget of money, of cold energy, and of acquisition and ownership. We exist to address exactly that.
