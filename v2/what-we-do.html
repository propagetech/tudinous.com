Our work model

From connection to performance

Tudinous Kryos maintains a large products and services database together with its true users. Here is how an engagement typically flows.

  1. Connect & understand

    We listen to your requirement and draw on our database of cryogenic users, products and services.

  2. Find & validate options

    We help you find different options for any cryogenic product or service, with validation of their performance reliability.

  3. Advise & collaborate

    When required, we arrange expert advice and knowledge support through collaboration with India's cryogenic experts.

  4. Coordinate procurement

    Once you finalise your choice, we assist with all coordination right up to your institution's procurement formalities.

  5. Support decisions

    We support your decision-making and follow-up actions through to successful implementation of the project.

  6. Secure value for money

    We represent a variety of organisations so you obtain the best price, offers that are economical, reliable and fit to purpose.
