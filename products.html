Our cryogenic product range

Tudinous aims to support you in selecting the right product according to your requirement. Below is the range we represent.

valve

Cryogenic valves

Globe, check and control valves engineered for low-temperature service, represented through Cryo Comp.

compress

Vacuum systems & components

Vacuum systems and components for insulation and cryogenic process applications.

precision_manufacturing

Cryogen delivery for cryo-machining

Delivery systems that supply cryogen precisely where it is needed for cryogenic machining.

science

Cryostats (incl. cryogen-free)

Cryostats for research and industry, including cryogen-free cryostats.

thermostat

Temperature controllers

Cryogenic temperature controllers for precise, stable low-temperature control.

conveyor_belt

Cryogenic transfer lines

Vacuum-insulated transfer lines for efficient cryogen distribution, represented through Demaco Holland.

propane_tank

Containers & storage

Cryogenic containers and storage facilities for safe handling of liquefied gases.

ac_unit

Cryocoolers

Cryocoolers and two-stage cryogenic cooling systems for closed-cycle refrigeration, including miniature Stirling cryocoolers from Global Sensor Technology (GSTiR), Wuhan.

factory

Liquefaction plants & upgrades

Small to medium-scale cryogenic liquefaction plants and upgrades for existing installations.

mode_fan

Fans & pumps

Cryogenic fans and pumps built for reliable operation at very low temperatures.

tune

Hydrogen & helium systems

Customised hydrogen and helium systems for specialised research and industrial needs.

gas_meter

Nitrogen & gas generation

Nitrogen systems specials, plus nitrogen, hydrogen and pure-air gas generation systems.
