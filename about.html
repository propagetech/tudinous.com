About Tudinous Kryos

Beautiful Cryogenics: the independent partner Indian cryogenic users can rely on.

Who we are

An esteemed cryogenics company, dedicated and unconventional

Tudinous Kryos Pvt. Ltd. is a private limited company registered with the Registrar of Companies, Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India, dedicated to the field of cryogenics, offering services that are unconventional, but useful and necessary.

The name says it all: Tudinous comes from the Latin word "pulchritudinous" meaning beautiful, and Kryos is the Greek word for cryogenics. Together, "Beautiful Cryogenics."

Our business model is built on an extremely large, acquired database of cryogenic users across India: people and institutions we have stayed connected with, and engaged with through business, over many years.

Our founder

Dr. Tushar Bhowmick

Tudinous Kryos is the brainchild of Dr. Tushar Bhowmick, a known personality in the Indian cryogenic community for the last 30 years. With a strong academic background from IIT Kharagpur, he served as Business Head of Philips Cryogenics and Stirling Cryogenics for two decades, and is a designated Fellow and active executive member of the Indian Cryogenic Council.

M.Sc. Physics · M.Tech Cryogenic Engineering · Ph.D Cryogenic Engineering, IIT Kharagpur

Our philosophy

Economic, reliable & fit to purpose

Cryogenic usage and R&D are always full of uncertainties: component failure, cold losses, repetitive experimentation, and unconventional physical and chemical processes. As a result, "budget" is an unavoidable word of concern.

We address this through a single, consistent philosophy, and we support our customers in their research, component selection and purchase decisions to make the choice that is best suited to their assignments and objectives.
