About Tudinous Kryos

Tudinous Kryos Pvt. Ltd. is a new born esteemed Pvt. Limited Company registered under the “Registrar of Companies”, Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Govt. of India and is dedicated to the field of cryogenics, offering services which are unconventional but useful and necessary.

The concept of such an organization is the brain child of Dr. Tushar Bhowmick, a known personality in Indian Cryogenic Community for last 30 years. With a strong back ground in academics (M.Sc. – Physics, M.Tech – Cryogenic Engg., Ph.D – Cryogenic Engineering) from reputed institute IIT – Kharagpur, Dr. Bhowmick continuously remain present in Indian Cryogenics arena as Business Head of Philips Cryogenics and Stirling Cryogenics for last 20 years. Dr. Bhowmick is also a designated Fellow and active executive member of Indian Cryogenic Council.

Tudinous business model is primarily based upon the extremely large acquired data base of Cryogenic Users in India with whom we remain connected and even engaged through business at different times.

Cryogenic usage activity (including research and development) is always full of uncertainties in terms of component failure, cold losses, repetitive experimentation and unconventional physical and chemical processes involved. As a result “ Budget ” is an important word of concern whether it is “ Budget of money ”, “ Budget of cold energy ”, “ Budget of Acquisition and ownership ” etc.

Tudinous wants to address this important but unavoidable aspect through its business philosophy :

ECONOMIC, RELIABLE & FIT TO PURPOSE

Tudinous will support its customers in their research process, component selection and purchase decision which will be best suited for their assignments / objectives.
