## ABOUT US (verbatim themes)

- New Pvt. Ltd. company dedicated to cryogenics, offering services that are "unconventional but
  useful and necessary."
- The concept is the brainchild of Dr. Tushar Bhowmick (credentials above).
- Business model is based on an extremely large acquired database of cryogenic users in India.
- Cryogenic R&D is full of uncertainties (component failure, cold losses, repeat experimentation,
  unconventional physical/chemical processes), so **"Budget"** matters — budget of **money**, of
  **cold energy**, of **acquisition and ownership**.
- Philosophy in capitals: **ECONOMIC, RELIABLE & FIT TO PURPOSE.**
- Tudinous supports customers in research, component selection and purchase decisions best suited to
  their objectives.

## WHAT WE DO (work model)

1. Maintains a large products/services database together with its true users.
2. Helps prospective cryogenic users find options for any required product/service, **with
   validation of performance reliability**; arranges expert advice via collaboration; represents
   supplier groups to connect them to customers.
3. Once a user finalises a choice, assists with all coordination until procurement formalities.
4. Supports decision-making and all follow-up until successful project implementation.
5. Helps users obtain the best price (value for money) by representing a variety of cryogenic
   product/service organisations whose offers are economical, reliable and fit to purpose.

- **Resources:** collaborating relationships with many reputed companies (quality products + expert
  teams) plus all willing Cryogenic Expertise of India; new collaborations formed as the market
  requires.
- **Commitment:** focused on the business philosophy; "from Proof of Concept to Performance";
  collaborative basis → more reliable than a single entity.
- **Relation:** long-term customer relationships built on **trust and mutual cooperation with
  respect** — visible in action, not just documents.
